Book a service that is suited to your motorcycle or scooter and our mechanic will come to you and deliver the service in your parking.

1.Choosing the right service You may book a service through the home page or the Book Now button. You can opt to avail a periodic service, repair services, fix accidental damages, tyre replacement or battery replacement. If you don’t know what’s’ wrong with your motorcycle or scooter, you can opt to get an inspection done.

2.Fill-up the vehicle details and select location We cater to all kinds of makes and models (less than 500 CC) across all brands including Honda, Hero, Bajaj, TVS, Suzuki, Yamaha, Royal Enfield, Piaggio, Aprilia. Please ensure you select the right variant. The make, model and location help us show you the right price for the service

3.Select the Plan (options if available) Once the pricing is available, select to book now. For periodic service, we offer multiple packages that are suitable to your use of the motorcycle or scooter. Select the plan that’s applicable for you.

4.Select the Date & Time Our services are not available on the same day. Service bookings on your preferred date and time are subject to slot availability. You may fill in your preferred date & time. In case the preferred slot is not available, our service team will contact you to reschedule

5.Additional Details If you have specific requirements for any spares or repairs, do mention it here, as it would help us to bring the necessary spares at the time of appointment. In case you have some problems with the vehicle and don’t know what to ask for, just type the complaint or symptoms you experience. This is important as it helps us diagnose the problem and help you with fixing the problem on the first visit.

6.Customer KYC Please ensure, you provide all the details accurately like the mobile number, alternate number, address and email id. Please do provide your email id so that we can send the status updates, estimate approvals and invoice details to you.

7.Booking Done! Once the booking is done, you shall receive a confirmation SMS & Email.
